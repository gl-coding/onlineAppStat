【App Description】
Tired of bloated browsers full of ads and news feeds?

ZenSiteGo is a back-to-basics web browser. No news feeds, no pop-ups, no tracking — just your favorite sites on a clean home screen, one tap away.

— Why ZenSiteGo? —

Minimalism is a choice.
We believe a browser should do one thing well: get you to the web pages you want, faster.

— Key Features —

▸ Card-Style Bookmark Panel
  Your favorite sites displayed as beautiful cards on the home screen — tap and go

▸ Drag & Drop Reordering
  Long press any card to rearrange — organize your sites like icons on your desktop

▸ One-Tap Quick Bookmark
  Save any page to your home panel while browsing — instant access next time

▸ Popular Site Suggestions
  Built-in recommendations for popular sites — get started in seconds

▸ Easy Sharing
  Found something great? Share it instantly to any app

▸ Privacy First
  All bookmarks and browsing data stored locally on your device — no collection, no upload, no tracking

▸ Clean Experience
  No ads · No push notifications · No sign-up · No background activity · Under 20MB

— Who is it for? —

• Efficiency-minded users who just want quick access to a few key sites
• A simple, easy-to-use browser for elderly family members
• Minimalists who don't want to be distracted by feeds and ads
• Anyone who needs a clean, lightweight backup browser

ZenSiteGo — Less is more.

【Review Notes】
This app is a minimal web browser with bookmark management, drag-and-drop reordering, and quick-save features. It requires NSAllowsArbitraryLoads to load user-specified URLs. All user data is stored locally on the device and is never uploaded to any server.
